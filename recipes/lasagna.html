Lasagna

Recipe

Homemade Lasagna is a classic that every cook should have in their rotation. Tender sheets of pasta, a cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce make the perfect dish!Homemade Lasagna is a classic that every cook should have in their rotation. Tender sheets of pasta, a cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce make the perfect dish!

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • ground beef
  • garlic
  • pasta sauce
  • egg

Steps

  1. oven to 350 and cook pasta al dente
  2. brown beef and garlic until no pink remains
  3. stir in pasta sauce and simmer 5 minutes
  4. make cheese mixture, combine 1.5 cups mozzarella and 0.25 cups parmesan cheese
  5. Layer pasta, meat and cheese
  6. cover with foil and bake 45 minutes
  7. uncover and sprinkle cheese on top
  8. rest 10-15 minutes
