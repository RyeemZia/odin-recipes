Berry Smoothie

Smoothies are a great way to get your daily servings of fruit. THey are delicios and quick to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Frozen Blueberries
  • 0.25 cup Frozen Raspberries
  • 1.25 cups Almond Milk
  • Half a medium size banana

Steps

  1. Add Almond milk to blender
  2. Add fruits to blender
  3. Blend until smoothie is liquified
  4. Pour into glass and enjoy
