Healthy French Fries

French fries are a classic. They are easy to make and very tasty. Plus these are healthy.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
    • Avocado Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat air fryer to 400 degrees farenheit
  2. Cut potatoes into wedges
  3. Salt to taste
  4. Massage 1 tsp of oil onto wedges
  5. Put in air fryer for 15 min
  6. Flip them half way through
  7. Take them out and let cool
  8. Enjoy
